Government Incentives

Explore purchase subsidies, tax exemptions, and registration incentives provided by national and state governments in India.

National Incentives

FAME India Scheme (Faster Adoption & Manufacturing of EVs)

  • Direct Purchase Subsidies: Direct subsidies linked to the battery capacity (e.g., ₹10,000 per kWh) for qualified vehicles.
  • Reduced GST Rates: Goods and Services Tax (GST) on electric vehicles is capped at a low 5% compared to up to 28% for combustion vehicles.
  • Registration Exemption: Road tax and registration fees are completely waived at national levels for pure electric drivetrains.
  • Income Tax Deductions: Individual tax deductions of up to ₹1.5 Lakhs on interest paid for electric vehicle loans (Section 80EEB).
